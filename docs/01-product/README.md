# Product Vision and Business Model

## Product Vision

Alpha Dragon aims to become the canonical layer for engineering intent and parametric design across multiple CAD ecosystems.

## Problem Statement

Engineering teams often face friction when moving from specification to model, especially when the design must be reused across multiple CAD tools, standards and manufacturing systems.

## Product Goals

- Create a universal engineering model independent of CAD software.
- Support parametric design and automation.
- Enable future AI-assisted design workflows.
- Provide a robust plugin and adapter ecosystem.

## Business Model Direction

The platform may evolve through:
- SaaS for collaborative engineering workflows,
- enterprise licensing for engineering teams,
- plugin marketplace and adapter ecosystem,
- API and SDK offerings for third-party integrations.

## MVP Focus

The first version should focus on the domain core: engineering project, assembly, part, body, geometry kernel, parametric engine, sketch and validation.
